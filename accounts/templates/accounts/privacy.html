Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 19, 2025

1. Introduction

StreamForge ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Full name, email address, and encrypted password when you register.
  • Payment Information: Processed securely via Stripe, Coinbase Commerce, or other PCI-compliant gateways. We never store full card numbers or crypto private keys.
  • Order Data: Music links (Spotify, Audiomack, etc.), selected countries, order quantities, and timestamps.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and activity logs for security and anti-fraud purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process payments and deliver ordered streams
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized use
  • To send service-related notifications (order status, balance alerts)
  • To improve our platform and customer support

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We only share data with:

  • Payment processors (Stripe, Coinbase, etc.) – encrypted & PCI-compliant
  • Trusted delivery partners who execute streaming campaigns (they only receive the track URL and targeting instructions – never your name or email)
  • Legal authorities if required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption in transit
  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt
  • Regular security audits and DDoS protection
  • Limited employee access with 2FA

6. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary:

  • Account data: until you request deletion
  • Order history & transactions: 7 years (for tax & refund purposes)
  • Logs: maximum 90 days

7. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Request a copy of your data
  • Request correction or deletion of your account
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential processing

Contact us at support@streamforge.io – we’ll respond within 48 hours.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies for login sessions and security. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Major changes will be announced via email or dashboard notification.

10. Contact Us

Email: support@streamforge.io
We typically reply within a few hours.
